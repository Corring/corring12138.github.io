		The configuration of the five forces differs by industry.
the strongest competitive force or forces determine the profitability of an industry and become the most important to strategy formulation.
有时候最sailent force不一定最明显。
industry structure grows out of a set of ECN and TECH chara that determine the strength of each competitive force.
(是ECN 和TECH的不同导致每个industry的竞争不同)


Threat of entry 
会带来新的竞争对手，以此来put a cap on the prodit potential of an industry。
竞争者如果在其他领域小有成就，会带着更多的资金来击垮你。
对策： barriers to entry
1. sypply-side ECN of scale (larger volumes enjoy lower costs per unit/ research / customer marketing / logistic system / etc.)
2. demand-side benefits of scale (buyer willingness to pay your product) reduceing the price the newcomer can command until it builds up a large base of customers.
3. customer switching costs 
4. capital requirements (invest large financial resources for fixed facilities / build inventories and fund startup losses) high return 可能会降低这一障碍
5. Incumbency advantage independent of size (no matter the size, incumbents may have cost or quality advantages e.g. proprietary technology / 
	preferencetial access access to the best MTL source / preemption of the most favorable geographic locations / established brand ID / cumlative experience to produce efficiently)
6. unequal access to distribution channels (the more limited the wholesale or retail channels are and the more that existing competitors have tied them up, the tougher entry into an industry)
	sometimes access to distribution is so high a barrier that new entrants must bypass distribution channels altogether or create their own
7. restrictive GVN policy (hinder or aid new entry directly & amplify the other entry barriers) e.g. licensing requirements and restructoins on foreign investment / safety regulation
	当然这也可以让entry变得容易
Expected retaliation 预测现有公司的反击也会影响新进入的公司是否要进入 Incumbents oftenuse public statements and responses to one entrant to send a message to other prospective entrants abt
	their commitment to defending MKT share. 新人可能会对你之前的反击害怕 / 你可能有钱或资源跟他竞争 / 价格战 / 行业上升程度慢很难做大 
Analysis of barriers to entry and expected retaliation 是对新人的阻挡



The power of suppliers
开价更高，质量更低，服务更差，或者给你的竞争对手那里转移你的资金。
如果 it is more concentrated than the industry it sells / supplier对你的依赖度不重 / 换供应商会非常的困难 / 供应商的货物很differentiated / 没有可替换物 / 压榨过头的联合反抗
	时，power of supplier会变大



The Power of Buyers
想要更多的value,更好的服务，更棒的质量。
if they have negotiating leverage relative to industry participants, buyers are more powerful. 尤其对于价格敏感性用户
如果顾客群体 少量的顾客或者一个顾客对一个供应商进行大量的购买 / 商品不differentiated / switching cost is low / vendor转的中间价太高直接绕过你进行购买 
	时power of buyer 会很大
一个顾客群体会是价格敏感性的当 顾客购买的物品cost structure 或者 procurement budget占其比重很大（没啥技术含量的东西） / 顾客资金不足或者盈利不高 / 服务和质量对于产品不是很重要
	 / 对于focus on price的顾客其他的花费影响不大 
不同产业里顾客间的差别不大，唯一的区别是their needs can be intangible and harder to quantify.
中间商和其他的买家使用的分析方法是相同的，当他们可以左右终端顾客的思想时他们的买家力会上升。减少中介会给producer带来更大的利益



The Threat of Substitutes
可代替物威胁 profit & reduce the bonanza an industry can reap in good times (growth potential)
substitute products or services limit an industry's profit potential by placing a ceiling on prices. Distance itself from substitutes.
Threat 很高如果 it offer an attractive price-performance trade-off to the industry's product / cost of switching to the substitute is low



Rivalry Aming Existing Competitors
price discounting, new product introductions, AD campaigns and service improvements
limit the profitability of an industry depend on intensity with the companies compete and on the basis on which they compete. 
Intensity is great if: 许多竞争者或者竞争者相同大小 / 行业增长缓慢导致MKT share fights / exit barriers are high 尽管没钱赚还是要继续 / 竞争者对BUS 很坚定并很坚定的想成为领导 e.g. 国有企业
	想要更加有威望和提高就业率
Rivalry对profitability是非常有破坏性的如果它们针对价格进行竞争
价格竞争在以下情况容易发生： 竞争者间的product几乎一样并且switching cost低 / FC很高然而MC很低 导致竞争者降价以此来卖的多来弥补FC / capacity must be expanded in large increments to be efficient
	/ 商品容易变质需要尽快出售 导致降价以达成目的
对除了价格的竞争不太可能会影响你的利润 e.g. product feature, support services, delivery time, or brand image 因为他们提供价值并support higher prices
当所有的人都竞争same dimensions,就会变成zero-sum competition； 但是rivalry可以是positive sum, 当大家服务不同的customer segments时其实就是提升了行业平均利润。
shift the nature of competition in a more positive direction




Factors, Not Forces
Five forces = overall structure 
尤其重要的是，要避免将某个行业的某些可见属性误认为其基础结构
1. Industry growth rate 在mute rivalry的同时也可以put suppliers in a powerful position 并且high growth with low entry abrriers will draw in entrants. 行业增长不等于利润增长
2. technology and innovation 先进的科技并不使行业变得更加吸引或不吸引人
3. GVN 政府的介入并不代表可利润性，最好的方法是去针对某一个政策来评估
4. Complementary products and services e.g. SW + HW 比 分开售卖更有利可图
